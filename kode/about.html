About Me

Let's get to know each other!


My name is Dominic Aramis, a 16 year old student, currently pursuing ITMP at Kuben VGS. I enjoy coding, drawing, gaming and writing, and I'm always seeking to expand my skills. I consider myself creative, and I tend to spend a lot of my free time drawing characters that I've made, or from shows and games I like. I also am interested in photography and taking memorable photos.

Why take IT?

I started in IT because I'm really interested in technology, gaming, coding and using my creative mind to code and create the things I envision. I'm a firm believer in that with the help of technology, anything we can think, we can create.

My Future Outlook

In the future I aspire to be a video game programmer/coder. That's something I think I would like working as, or at least something creative. I firmly enjoy being creative as a hobby, but I'd also want to pursue it in a career as well.
